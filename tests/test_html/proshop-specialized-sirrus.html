Details

Speed, comfort and confident handling come together to make Specialized's Sirrus a carefree ride. It sports a light aluminum frame, a pavement-smoothing steel fork and efficient, road bike-size 700c wheels for easy pedaling on every road and path. Shimano's 24-speed drivetrain provides a wide range of gears for easy climbing, while the powerful linear-pull brakes control your speed on the fun back down. And thanks to the flat handlebars and comfort-oriented riding position, you'll finish every ride feeling great.

Comments:

Bought this to replace a Mongoose mountain bike I've used for 10 yrs. Put on my gel seat and got out of subway (Kings Highway) in Bklyn to drive to Marine Pkwy Bridge. Ended up taking a 3 1/2 hr ride and stopping at Tacoway Beach for a bite in the Rockaways! I took the bike off-road on purpose to see if I should give away my MB. The tires on the Sirrus are smaller than MB tires but thicker than road bike tires so if you're NOT going over logs and boulder, you'll be more than fine. Most of my 40 min rides are on pavement or gravel and than short sandy, pebbly areas so this bike is perfect! Subway stairs are a bit of a hassle as I have to lift the front of the bike by handlebars as I descend/ascend stairs. To get a really light bike is 3 times or more and for the price this is a bike I can ride around NYC and lock up in the daytime and feel secure. Steers easily around pot holes but don't ride over sewer grates. Great purchase . . . fun to ride!!!
