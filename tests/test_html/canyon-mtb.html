Highlights

Developed to be raced at the highest levels of the Enduro World Series, the Strive CFR 9.0 Team featuring the new-and-improved Shapeshifter is our answer to the ever-increasing demands of Enduro racing. Designed for the racers of the Canyon Factory Enduro Team, if the podium is your goal, the Strive CFR 9.0 Team is the bike for you. Even lighter than its predecessor with the new CFR frame, its long reach ensures you always stay in control and keep hold of those decisive seconds no matter how gnarly the trail gets.
