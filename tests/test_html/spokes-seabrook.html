Details

The Seabrook’s laid-back, feet flat-on-the-ground geometry makes for a confidence-inspiring ride. There’s no need to step-off the saddle when you stop, just put your feet flat on the ground. With 8 speeds, a lightweight aluminum frame, 26" wheels, and super-comfortable saddle, it’s quite possibly the perfect bike.

- Custom aluminum frame
- Shimano Tourney 8-speed shifting
- 26" alloy wheels with stainless-steel spokes
