Details

Perfect for trips to the store or a leisurely stroll through the park, the Fitzroy has the right amount of front wheel drive power to put a little extra spring in your stroke. Designed and tested in Denmark, the Promovec drive system is simple to use, easy to maintain, and provides hours of e-assisted cycling fun.

- Pro-Movec 250W front hub drive system
- Rack Mounted 7.8Ah smart battery
- Shimano Tourney 7-Speed shifting
- Tektro V-Brakes
- 700c wheels
- Aluminum frame
