Details

Graceful steel tubing, finely tuned suspension, and 27.5-inch wheels—it's easy to see why Jamis' Dragonfly is known as a legend. A Reynolds 853 chromoly frame ensures a smooth, solid ride for years of fun trail riding. A tapered Fox Float with a 15mm thru-axle enables precision steering with 120mm of trail-taming suspension. WTB tubeless-ready 27.5-inch wheels roll over rough terrain and carry plenty of momentum on this ride-anything hardtail. Shimano disc brakes bestow confidence and control, while Shimano's smooth 20-speed drivetrain delivers snappy shifting and the right gears to rocket you up the climbs. Ritchey finishes off the Dragonfly with stylish, lightweight components.
