    Unicoi Endurance XC

    The Unicoi provides unparalleled performance, versatility and simplicity.

    Named after the trail-rich northeast region of Tennessee, the Unicoi has been ridden over the years in some of the most prominent cross country races, including the Olympics and UCI World Cup. The new Unicoi has evolved from a deep history of unmatched performance and attention to detail.

    The Unicoi's damped hardtail strut design helps keep the traction to the ground while climbing, flowing through single track and tackling descents. The simple design also saves weight when compared to full suspension models.

    Litespeed's proprietary Uni-Strut dual spring system is designed to control the compression damping and reduces rapid rebound & top out, providing 45mm (1¾ in) of rear travel that is best combined with a 120mm travel fork. The Unicoi is built with Litespeed's carbon chainstays to stiffen the bike while still retaining its high performance.

    Key Features

    • Damped hardtail with 45mm of rear travel
    • Uni-Strut suspension design
    • Encapsulated coil spring system
    • 3AL/2.5V titanium frame & Litespeed carbon chainstay
    • Oversized 44mm head tube
    • 29" tire up to 29x2.4"
    • 27.5"+ tire up to 27.5x3.0"
    • Optimized for 100-140mm travel fork
    • Internal dropper seat post routing
    • Sealed cartridge bearings in pivots
    • PF30 BB shell
    • Intended use: marathon, cross country
    • Handmade in the USA
    • Average weight for size medium frame - 2,609 grams

    • Find answers to common questions about bike builds, custom component selections, and shipping. If you have any questions feel free to live-chat with us or contact us.

      How long does it take to receive my bike?
      Most frame and frameset orders ship in 1-2 business days. Full bike orders can be expected to ship in 1-2 weeks time and this varies per bike model, size, and build kit. For an accurate delivery estimate live-chat contact us!

      Can I make changes to the build kit?
      Absolutely. Our bikes are built to order so you can customize however you'd like. If there are components you'd really like on your bike but don't see them offered live-chat or contact us for availability and a custom quote!

      Can I have a different length stem, crankarms, or different gearing?
      We spec different length components on our bikes based on common lengths per each size of bike. If you are looking to specify what length components or the gearing you'd like on your new Litespeed just live-chat or contact us and we'll get you started on a custom order.

      How much does shipping cost?
      UNITED STATES: Shipping for frame and frameset orders is $55 USD. For full bike orders shipping is $195 and includes professional assemble at our Litespeed factory and shipment in our Litespeed Pro Box so you don't have to assemble the bike when it arrives to your door.

      OUTSIDE OF USA: Shipping for frame and frameset orders is $95 USD. For full bike orders shipping is $195 - this includes semi-pro assembly and ships in our more compact International Shipping Box for safe keeping.

      Does my bike arrive assembled?
      For orders in the United States our bikes ship assembled in our Litespeed Pro Box. Adjust your bars then add the seatpost and front wheel and you're ready to ride.
