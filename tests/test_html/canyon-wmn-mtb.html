WMN MountainbikeS

From ultra-lightweight hardtails to full suspension trail tamers, our women’s range of mountain bikes take all of the innovations from our unisex models and optimize them for the female rider. With unique specs and specially tuned suspensions across four different platforms, get out there and Find Your Best!

What is WMN?

All Canyon WMN bikes are created solely with female riders in mind. What does this mean? We focus on what’s most important for women buying a bike: female-specific contact points combined with well-considered component specs. By doing this we can enhance our bikes' performance to offer women at any level, from beginner cyclist to seasoned competitors, the best possible riding experience.
